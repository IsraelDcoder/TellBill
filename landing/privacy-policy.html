Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

TellBill ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, company details, business address
  • Profile Information: Company logo, website, tax ID, payment settings
  • Invoice Data: Client names, addresses, project details, payment information
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our support channels
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe and RevenueCat (we do not store card details)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, frequency of use, actions performed, time spent in app
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Analytics Data: Via Sentry and analytics services to track performance and errors
  • Voice Data: Only stored locally on your device when using voice-to-invoice feature

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our Service
  • Processing invoices and managing your account
  • Sending transactional emails (invoice confirmations, payment reminders)
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and functionality
  • Customer support and troubleshooting

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, RevenueCat (for billing)
  • Email Service: Resend (for transactional emails)
  • SMS Providers: Twilio (for SMS reminders with consent)
  • Analytics: Sentry (error tracking), Firebase (analytics)
  • Customer Support: Intercom (for chat support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court orders

All third-party partners are contractually obligated to maintain data confidentiality and use data only for the purposes we specify.

5. Data Security

TellBill implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • PostgreSQL database with encrypted backups
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • No storage of credit card information (delegated to Stripe)
  • Sentry integration for real-time error monitoring and security alerts
Note: While we strive to protect your data, no security system is impenetrable. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our Service and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Account Deletion: After 30 days of account deletion, data is permanently removed
  • Backups: Backup copies are retained for 90 days for disaster recovery
  • Legal Hold: Data may be retained if legally required

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are a resident of the European Union, the following additional rights apply:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of marketing communications
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: Contact your local data protection authority

Legal Basis: We process your data based on: (1) Your consent, (2) Contract performance, (3) Legal compliance, (4) Legitimate business interests.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

TellBill uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login information (session cookies)
  • Track analytics and usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Personalize content and recommendations

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

TellBill is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Your Rights & Choices

  • Marketing Emails: Click "Unsubscribe" in any email
  • SMS Notifications: Reply "STOP" to opt out
  • Account Data: Access or download your data via account settings
  • Account Deletion: Request account deletion at support@tellbill.com

11. Data Processing & Compliance

TellBill complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • PIPEDA (Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
  • Industry-specific regulations for financial data

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located in the United States. By using TellBill, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@tellbill.com
  • Mailing Address: TellBill, Inc. | Support Team | United States
  • Response Time: We will respond within 30 days

14. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" timestamp. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
